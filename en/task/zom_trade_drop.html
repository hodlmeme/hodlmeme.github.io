ZOM Trading Airdrop

When you lose, it's time for ZOO OF MEME to airdrop.

Number of airdrops

5% HODL total supply airdrop

Rules

  1. The event will last for 15 days, from 2 AM (UTC) on May 9th to 2 AM (UTC) on May 24th.
  2. Airdrops will be distributed based on the trading volume and listings of ZOME NFTs.
  3. The top 10% of users ranked by listing quantity (quantity x amount) will share 5% of the total airdrop.
  4. Trading users will share 95% of the total airdrop based on their proportion of trading volume.
  5. The event is only applicable to Opensea and OKX NFT marketplaces.
