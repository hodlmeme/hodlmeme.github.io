WhiteList

Types

  • Guaranteed White
  • Non-Guaranteed White

Acquisition Methods

  • Participate in Discord group activities
  • Raffles hosted by major Key Opinion Leaders (KOLs)
  • Tweet and get retweeted by the founder

Purpose

After obtaining the whitelist, apply for redeeming a box by creating a ticket in the Discord group.

  1. Open the Discord group, find "SUPPORT" and then "congratulations".
  2. Click“Create ticket”
  3. As shown in the image, the ticket creation is successful. After clicking, directly send your application in the chat window.
  4. Wait patiently for the administrator's review results as there may be a large number of tickets. Generally, you will receive a result within 1-2 days. After the review, click "close" to close the ticket.
