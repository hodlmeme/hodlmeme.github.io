Box

Box Types

There are three types of boxes, which are:

  1. OG Box
  2. BUILDER Box
  3. HODL Box (Total quantity unknown)
  4. Heartfelt Box
  5. AiDoge-Box

OG Box

Total Quantity：10,000, Each box contains 945,000,000,000 $HODL.

Note: There are three ways to open the box, which will affect the amount of $HODL you will receive

Opening the box will receive HODL, for 4.5% HODL supply.

The OG Box can be traded in the market, or you can choose to open it to obtain $HODL.

Methods of Acquisition:

  1. Donations
  2. [Crowdfunding(/task/crowd.md)
  3. Participate in events and exchange for it with a Whitelist(/award/whitelist.md)

BUILDER Box

Total Quantity：500,000, Each box contains 19,274,163,088.849 $HODL.

Without a lock-up period, you will receive 4.5% of $HODL.

Methods of Acquisition
1. Will be distributed to early contributors
2. Will be airdropped to holders of Dayou's Friend Tech Club Hodler

Before moving towards DAO, the team did not take even a penny from the burn tax for personal gain. In the subsequent DAO plan, to ensure the greatness of the HODL project, the team gave up their share of 20% of the burn tax and distributed it to the community. Therefore, the team will be rewarded with a one-time 50% allocation of Builder boxes. Among them, Dayou accounts for 50% of the team's share, and approximately 25,000 Builder boxes (20%) will be airdropped to holders of Dayou's Friend Tech Club Hodler.

Open The Box

https://hodlmeme.io/#/open-boxes

There are three ways to open the boxes:

  1. Open now and receive 35% of the tokens with 1x mining points.
  2. Open linearly over 7 days and receive 70% of the tokens with 5x mining points.
  3. Open linearly over 10 days and receive 100% of the tokens with 15x mining points.
