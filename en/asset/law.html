LAW

According to historical records, the day when human beings were born, there emerged the "Law," and some ancestors with pure beliefs obtained the "Law"...

law（CODE OF HODL）

total：2,100,000,000

"Law" is an NFT provided together with HODL during the initial mint. Strictly speaking, the "Law" NFT was minted first, and then the airdrop of HODL was received.

Contract address（CA）

0x1832e00dff829547e1f564f92401c2886f3236b4

Allocation

  1. Ancestors who participated in the genesis event
  2. Team reservation, the founder has distributed this portion entirely to the ancestors

Functions

  1. Burning "Law" can obtain "Contracts", https://law.hodlmeme.io/law
  2. Using "Law" to participate in auctions on ZOO OF MEME
  3. There will be a special reward pool in the later stages, which requires possession of "Law" to participate
  4. Other implied functions remain to be discovered

Mint

https://law.hodlmeme.io/home

Rule

  1. The participating wallet address must have a BABT
  2. Can mint 100 NFT per time, once/0.0128 BNB
  3. The maximum number of mints depends on the previous interaction of the address on the BSC

Results

  1. The BNB spent on minting has been fully refunded after the event ended.
  2. Participants obtained "Law".
  3. The remaining unsold "Law" was distributed to participants based on their participation ratio.
  4. Participants received an airdrop of $HODL.
  5. The founder distributed the team's share to the participants.
