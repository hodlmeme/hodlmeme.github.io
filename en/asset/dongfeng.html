ATLANTIS

"Atlantis" is the token of the "Federation" project, which is issued on BNB CHAIN. The main LP is Atlantis-HODL.

Atlantis

"Atlantis" originated from Plato's works, describing a nation founded by the son of Poseidon, the god of the sea. This nation achieved victory in its war against the Athenians, but due to the "unjust" nature of its victory, it was sunk beneath the sea. People have been searching for it ever since, and the Federation hopes to recreate this place on the blockchain.

Allocation

  • 70% will be placed in the "Federation Boxes." Upon the opening of the claim, eligible addresses will be allowed to receive them almost for free in the form of paying 1 USDT worth of BNB.   • 5% will be airdropped to addresses that lock their LP on the HODL official website after the third phase (May 27th, 4:00 am UTC).

  • 10% will be allocated through the ZOO OF MEME launchpad.
